2. Calculating Normal Distribution¶

(1) Creating normal distribution¶

To calculate probability of specific value to happen, we need to identify:

  1. Mean: m
  2. Standard Deviation: sd
  3. Number of Samples: n

Greer will guarantee that its engines will provide a certain amount of lifetime flight hours subject to the airline purchasing a preventive-maintenance service plan that is also provided by Greer.

With proper maintenance service, their engine’s lifetime flight hours achieved is normally distributed, mean=36500, SD=5000.

Question1.¶

They want to promote that once they purchase the maintenance service pack, then you don’t need to replace your engine until you use it for 40,000 hrs by XX%.

So, Greer would like to know what percentage of its aircraft engines will be expected to last more than 40,000hrs. That is, what is the probability that the aircraft lifetime flight hours x will exceed 40,000.

In [ ]:
# Calculating a probability that the aircraft lifetime flight hours x will exceed 40,000.
cum=stat.NormalDist(mu=36500, sigma=5000).cdf(40000)
print("A probability that the aircraft lifetime flight hours will exceed 40,000 is", round(1-cum,4)*100,"%")
